Imagen 4

Imagen 4 is Google's advanced image generation model designed for professional creative work. It is described as "a high-performance engine for advanced visual synthesis, offering sophisticated creative control and photorealistic output."

Supported Capabilities

  • High-fidelity photorealistic image generation
  • Advanced visual synthesis with creative control
  • Precise text rendering within images
  • Complex lighting and scene composition
  • Multiple image sizes: "1K" and "2K" (Standard/Ultra)
  • Aspect ratios: "1:1", "3:4", "4:3", "9:16", "16:9"
  • Person generation control

Use Cases

Recommended for:

  • Professional branding and marketing assets
  • Intricate scene composition
  • High-fidelity design tasks
  • Precise text rendering (infographics, menus, diagrams)
  • Complex lighting requirements
  • Portrait photography simulation
