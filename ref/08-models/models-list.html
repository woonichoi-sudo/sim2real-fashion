Gemini API Models Overview

Google's Gemini API documentation presents a comprehensive catalog of AI models across multiple categories.

Current Generation Models

Gemini 3 Series

  • Gemini 3.1 Pro (Preview) - Advanced intelligence for complex problem-solving, suited for agentic tasks.
  • Gemini 3.5 Flash (Stable) - "Most intelligent model for sustained frontier performance on agentic and coding tasks." Most capable model for sustained performance on coding and agentic work.
  • Gemini 3 Flash (Preview) - Frontier-class performance
  • Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite (Stable) - Cost-efficient option
  • Gemini 3.1 Flash Live (New Preview) - "High-quality, low-latency audio-to-audio model designed for real-time dialogue." Real-time dialogue support.
  • Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS (New Preview) - Speech generation with "natural outputs, steerable prompts"

Gemini 2.5 Series

  • Gemini 2.5 Flash (Stable) - Best price-performance for low-latency tasks
  • Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite (Stable) - Fastest, most budget-friendly
  • Gemini 2.5 Pro (Stable) - Most advanced for complex reasoning

Organizational Structure

Models are grouped by function:

  • Standard text/multimodal
  • Generative media (video and images)
  • Audio
  • Music generation
  • Specialized tools

Version Classification

The documentation clarifies four stability tiers:

  • stable: production-ready
  • preview: testing with billing
  • latest: automatic updates
  • experimental: rapid iteration with usage restrictions

Deprecated Models

Deprecated models from the Gemini 2.0 series are marked for shutdown, with "at least 2 weeks notice" before deprecation. Developers are encouraged to migrate to newer versions.
